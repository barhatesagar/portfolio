Manuguru HMIS (Health Management Information System)

Project Information

  • Category: Web Application
  • Client: Heavy Water Plant (Manuguru)
  • Project Date: 13 June, 2024

In the Manuguru HMIS project, I will be responsible for developing the entire application UI according to the specific requirements of the Manuguru client. The project includes the following key components:

  1. Pharmacy Medicine Module: This module will manage the inventory and distribution of medicines within the pharmacy, ensuring efficient tracking and availability.
  2. Out Pharmacy Module Changes: Implementing necessary changes to the out pharmacy module to enhance the process of handling medicine.
  3. Inventory Module Adjustments: Overseeing all required modifications to the inventory module to meet the evolving needs of the healthcare facility.
  4. Master Data Management for Inventory Module: Developing functionalities related to master data management within the inventory module to ensure accurate and efficient data handling.

My focus will be on creating a user-friendly, efficient, and compliant interface that meets the client's expectations and enhances the overall functionality of the HMIS.
